# Design Context — Qatar Restaurant Deals

## Target audience
Residents and expats in Qatar (mostly Doha) looking for restaurant dining deals. Mobile-first, bilingual (English + Arabic / RTL). They're deciding where to eat and want a good offer fast.

## Use cases
- Browse current dining offers and quickly scan what's worth it
- Filter by cuisine, area, deal type, **price**, and **dine-in vs delivery**
- See what's expiring soon / the biggest discounts (the featured carousel)
- Tap through to the source / the restaurant

## Brand personality / tone — "Editorial Luxe"
Warm, premium, refined — like a dining magazine, not a coupon-clipping site. It should feel a little upscale (fits Qatar's hotel fine-dining offers) while staying clear, fast, and trustworthy.

- **Palette:** warm bone/cream background, deep Qatar maroon, gold accent. Tinted warm neutrals. No pure black/white.
- **Type:** an elegant serif display (Fraunces) for headlines, a clean sans (Outfit) for body, Cairo for Arabic. No Inter/Roboto/system.
- **Layout:** airy, asymmetric, magazine rhythm. Not a monotonous card grid.
- **Signature moment:** an auto-rotating *featured deals* carousel ("wheel banner") of bold typographic cards.

## Hard constraints
- **No restaurant photos** (copyright) → design is driven by type, color, and layout, not imagery.
- Static Astro + Tailwind PWA on GitHub Pages; EN/AR + RTL; accessible; fast; $0; data scraped daily.
- Store only deal facts; link back to source.

## Differentiation
A deals app that looks like a premium Doha dining guide — typographic, warm, and confident — instead of a generic photo-grid coupon app.
